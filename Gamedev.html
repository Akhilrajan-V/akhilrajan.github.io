I have a proven track record of creating numerous immersive 3D, AR/VR games, and applications. My expertise extends to the realm of inverse kinematic based 3D character rigging. VR Game: Escape ROOM - D.o.D

Checkout the immersive VR game called Escape Room: Do.or.Die, developed by me with the help of two of my friends. As the name suggests it is an escape room game with 2 levels. The player has to solve a series of puzzles to progress through each level. Completion of all puzzles in one level leads to the next level.
Game is created using Unity.
